Tiantian Geng is a PhD student studying at the University of Birmingham (UoB) and Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), funded by SUSTech and supervised by Dr Jinming Duan and Dr Feng Zheng. Previously, She received her M.Eng. and B.Eng. from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2019 and 2022, respectively. Her research interests include computer vision, video understanding and multi-modal learning.
